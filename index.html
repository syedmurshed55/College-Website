We are offer to three best course retalted modern science for student by Moulvibazar Polytechnic Institute

COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY

The Computer Technology course is a full time four years (six semesters) Diploma Degree in Computer.This degree helps the students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer.

FOOD TECHNOLOGY

The Food Technology course is a full time four years (six semesters) Diploma Degree in Food. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as preservatibes, drink, and cakes.

ELECTRONIC TECHNOLOGY

The Electronic Technology course is a full time four years (six semesters) Diploma Degree in Electronics. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as electronic devices, Machines, and Programming.
